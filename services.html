Services

Custom hardwood, from prep to final coat.

Whether it's one refinished room or a full custom medallion floor, every job gets the same care for prep and attention. Here's everything we do.

01

Custom hardwood installation

Solid and engineered hardwood — European oak, walnut, ash and more — measured, milled and laid so the grain flows unbroken from wall to wall. Wide-plank or traditional, nailed or glued, finished on site for a seamless result.

  • Solid & engineered
  • Wide-plank
  • Long plank
  • On-site finishing
02

Herringbone, chevron & parquetry

Pattern work is where we shine. Herringbone, chevron, basket-weave and bespoke parquetry, each piece cut and aligned by hand for crisp, repeating geometry that turns an ordinary room into the centrepiece of the home.

  • Herringbone
  • Chevron
  • Parquetry
  • Basket-weave
03

Borders & inlays

One-of-a-kind detail. Contrasting wood borders with metal and resin inlays, hand-cut and set into the floor to frame a room — the fine work most crews won't attempt.

  • Contrasting borders
  • Copper & brass inlay
  • Resin & epoxy
  • Hand-set detail
04

Medallions & compass designs

Our signature craft. Every medallion and compass rose is hand-drawn and planned to your preferences, then hand-measured, hand-cut and hand-installed on site — crafted piece by piece. No two are ever alike, and none can be duplicated.

  • Hand-drawn design
  • Hand-cut
  • Crafted on site
  • One of a kind
05

Custom staircases

Mitred treads, custom landing designs and feature borders, finished to match the floor below. A staircase is the most-seen surface in a home — we treat it like the statement piece it is.

  • Mitred treads
  • Custom landing design
  • Matched finish
  • Feature borders
06

Sanding, staining & refinishing

Bring worn hardwood back to life. Precise sanding, custom stain matching and durable premium finishes — restoring depth and protection without replacing a single board where we don't have to.

  • Dustless sanding
  • Custom stain match
  • Durable finishes
  • Repairs
07

Subfloor preparation

Improper floor prep is the reason a floor can fail — unusual gaps, squeaks and cupping down the line. We grind, level, moisture-test and prep every subfloor properly, so what we build on top lasts decades.

  • Grinding
  • Leveling
  • Moisture testing
  • Proper prep

FAQ

The questions we hear most.

How much does a custom hardwood floor cost?+
Every estimate is priced to the project — species, square footage, pattern complexity and the amount of prep work all factor in. A simple refinish and a hand-laid medallion floor sit at very different points. Send us the details and we'll put together a clear, itemised estimate so you know exactly what you're paying for.
How long will my project take?+
It depends on size and complexity — a single refinished room can be a few days, while a full custom installation with pattern work takes longer. You'll get a realistic timeline with your estimate, and we keep you updated throughout.
Do you work outside of Arizona?+
Yes. We're based in Phoenix and serve all of Arizona — Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, Gilbert and beyond — and we travel nationwide for exceptional projects.
Can you refinish my existing floors instead of replacing them?+
Often, yes. If the boards are sound, sanding, custom stain matching and a fresh finish can bring worn hardwood back to life — without the cost of a full replacement. We'll tell you honestly which makes more sense for your floor.
Do you do herringbone, medallions and custom patterns?+
That's exactly what we're known for — herringbone, chevron, parquetry, contrasting borders, brass inlays and fully custom medallions, including showpieces built one piece at a time.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Yes — Arizona licensed, bonded and insured (ROC 342486), so your home and your investment are protected from start to finish.
