Services

Custom hardwood, from prep to final coat.

Whether it's one refinished room or a full custom medallion floor, every job gets the same care for prep, pattern and finish. Here's everything we do.

01

Custom hardwood installation

Solid and engineered hardwood — European oak, walnut, ash and more — measured, milled and laid so the grain flows unbroken from wall to wall. Wide-plank or traditional, nailed or glued, finished on site for a seamless result.

  • Solid & engineered
  • Wide-plank
  • On-site finishing
  • Hardwax oil
02

Herringbone, chevron & parquetry

Pattern work is where we shine. Herringbone, chevron, basket-weave and bespoke parquetry, each piece cut and aligned by hand for crisp, repeating geometry that turns an ordinary room into the centrepiece of the home.

  • Herringbone
  • Chevron
  • Parquetry
  • Basket-weave
03

Borders, inlays & medallions

One-of-a-kind detail. Contrasting wood borders, metal and resin inlays, and fully custom floor medallions — including 100-hour showpieces built one piece at a time. The kind of work most crews won't attempt.

  • Contrasting borders
  • Copper & brass inlay
  • Resin & epoxy
  • Custom medallions
04

Custom staircases

Mitred treads, inlaid landings and glass railings, finished to match the floor below. A staircase is the most-seen surface in a home — we treat it like the statement piece it is.

  • Mitred treads
  • Inlaid landings
  • Matched finish
  • Feature borders
05

Sanding, staining & refinishing

Bring tired hardwood back to life. Precise sanding, custom stain matching and durable hardwax-oil or polyurethane finishes — restoring depth and protection without replacing a single board where we don't have to.

  • Dustless sanding
  • Custom stain match
  • Hardwax oil
  • Repairs
06

Subfloor preparation

The unglamorous half most installers rush — and the reason floors gap, cup or squeak years later. We level, moisture-test and prep every subfloor properly, so what we build on top lasts decades.

  • Leveling
  • Moisture testing
  • Squeak elimination
  • Proper prep
Also available

Engineered wood, luxury vinyl & LVP, travertine, and custom wood detail for walls and ceilings. If it's a surface worth getting right, we'll talk it through with you.
