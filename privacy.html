Privacy Policy.

How Custom Flooring Projects LLC collects, uses and protects the information you share with us through this website.

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

Custom Flooring Projects LLC (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a custom hardwood flooring company based in Phoenix, Arizona. This policy applies to information collected through our website.

What we collect

We only collect the information you choose to send us. When you submit our estimate or contact form, that typically includes your name, phone number, email address, city and state, project timing, and any project details you provide. We do not ask for payment details or sensitive personal information through this site.

How we use it

We use your information solely to respond to your inquiry, prepare an estimate, and communicate with you about your project. We do not sell, rent or trade your information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Service providers

To operate the site we rely on a few trusted third parties: our web host delivers the pages, our form provider (FormSubmit) forwards your form submissions to our email inbox, and an embedded map service may load map imagery. These providers process data only as needed to provide their service.

Cookies & analytics

Our site uses minimal cookies. Any third-party content we embed (such as maps or social video) may set its own cookies under its own privacy policy.

Data retention & security

We keep inquiry emails only as long as needed to serve you and maintain our records, and we take reasonable measures to protect the information you send us. No method of transmission over the internet is ever completely secure.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct or delete the information you’ve sent us at any time — just email customflooringprojects@gmail.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at customflooringprojects@gmail.com or (848) 238-9299.
