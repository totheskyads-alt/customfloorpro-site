What sets us apart

We take on the floors most crews won't touch.

Herringbone, chevron, brass inlays, medallions, intricate borders and custom staircases — the detailed work that takes patience and the right hands. It's our specialty, not a side job.

One owner-led team handles everything: measuring, subfloor prep, supply, installation and finishing. No subcontractors and no shortcuts — just floors that still look flawless decades later.
