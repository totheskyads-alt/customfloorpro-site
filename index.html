The studio

A custom flooring company — not a flooring contractor.

Custom Flooring Projects is a high-end hardwood studio specializing in custom installations, herringbone and chevron patterns, parquetry, borders, medallions and staircases — the work most crews won't take on.

One owner-led team handles measure, subfloor preparation, supply, install and finish. We work exclusively with professional tools, and we travel for exceptional projects.

— Craftsmanship. Precision. Custom. Luxury.
What we do

Every detail underfoot, handled.

01

Custom hardwood installation

Solid & engineered oak, walnut and ash — measured, milled and laid so the grain runs unbroken.
02

Herringbone & chevron

Geometry that turns a room into a centrepiece. Every piece cut and aligned by hand.

Borders & medallions

Custom staircases
