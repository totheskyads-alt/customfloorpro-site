About

Obsessed with the details.

Custom Flooring Projects is an owner-led hardwood studio built on a simple belief: the floor deserves the same craftsmanship as everything that stands on it.

Our story

One craftsman, one standard, every floor.

Founded by Cristian Mocanu, Custom Flooring Projects grew out of a love for working with wood and a refusal to cut corners. What started as a one-man operation is now a trusted name for custom hardwood across Arizona.

We position ourselves as a custom flooring company — not a general contractor. The focus is luxury hardwood: bespoke designs, herringbone patterns, medallions, borders and the kind of craftsmanship you only notice because it's flawless.

We work exclusively with professional tools, offer clear estimates and honest communication, and tailor every solution to the client's preferences, needs and budget.

— laid by hand, signed by name.
