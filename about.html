About

Obsessed with the details.

Custom Flooring Projects is an owner-led hardwood studio built on a simple belief: the floor deserves the same craftsmanship as everything that stands on it.

Our story

It started with one craftsman.

Custom Flooring Projects started with Cristian Mocanu, a love of working with wood, and a refusal to cut corners. What began as a one-man operation is now a trusted name for custom hardwood across Arizona — and beyond.

We focus on the hard stuff: herringbone and chevron, hand-cut inlays, medallions, borders and statement staircases — the craftsmanship you only really notice because nothing about it looks wrong.

Cristian still attends training and industry classes to keep sharpening his craft — we focus on a high-skilled, custom-trained crew. Clear estimates, detailed quotations, honest communication, professional tools and materials, and a finish tailored to your space and budget. One crew, accountable from the first measurement to the last coat.

FAQ

The questions we hear most.

How much does a custom hardwood floor cost?+
Every estimate is priced to the project — species, square footage, pattern complexity and the amount of prep work all factor in. A simple refinish and a hand-laid medallion floor sit at very different points. Send us the details and we'll put together a clear, itemised estimate so you know exactly what you're paying for.
How long will my project take?+
It depends on size and complexity — a single refinished room can be a few days, while a full custom installation with pattern work takes longer. You'll get a realistic timeline with your estimate, and we keep you updated throughout.
Do you work outside of Arizona?+
Yes. We're based in Phoenix and serve all of Arizona — Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, Gilbert and beyond — and we travel nationwide for exceptional projects.
Can you refinish my existing floors instead of replacing them?+
Often, yes. If the boards are sound, sanding, custom stain matching and a fresh finish can bring worn hardwood back to life — without the cost of a full replacement. We'll tell you honestly which makes more sense for your floor.
Do you do herringbone, medallions and custom patterns?+
That's exactly what we're known for — herringbone, chevron, parquetry, contrasting borders, brass inlays and fully custom medallions, including showpieces built one piece at a time.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Yes — Arizona licensed, bonded and insured (ROC 342486), so your home and your investment are protected from start to finish.
